# Image Generation Prompt

Concept visualization only. Do not treat generated images as dimensional, tuning, CAD, DXF, or fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photograph of a simple cylindrical single-reed clarinet study instrument, understated and prototype-like rather than concert-grade: dark hardwood or black-stained wooden body, plain bell, clean mouthpiece and reed, mostly keyless body with a few clearly provisional tone-hole closures or removable test pads, corked joints, and a measured-build lab aesthetic. Place it on a clean gray studio surface with calipers and a blank measurement card as scale cues, soft directional studio lighting, three-quarter product-photography angle, about a 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop scene of the clarinet study laid beside bore-station templates, reed and mouthpiece samples, leak-test tubing, and a tuner on a quiet bench. Natural light from the side, realistic dust and wood texture, the instrument appears as an early measurement-first clarinet-like prototype with cylindrical bore study intent, no finished concert keywork, environmental storytelling about validation and measurement.

## Detail/Macro Prompt

Close-up macro photograph of the reed and mouthpiece end of the clarinet study: cane reed on a plain black mouthpiece, cork tenon, smooth cylindrical wood body, one nearby tone-hole test closure, tiny leak-test witness marks, soft highlights on polished wood and cork grain, photorealistic, high-detail.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid brand logos, watermarks, readable text, full Boehm-system keywork, fake maker marks, impossible pads, extra limbs, and any visual that implies build-ready measured hole placement.
